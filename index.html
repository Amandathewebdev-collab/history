JULY 20TH

On July 20th, the world witnessed powerful moments that changed history forever
Did you know Juy 20th is not just a moon landing day? It's also celebrated as international chess day, chosen
by FIDE in 1966 to honour the birth of international chess federation back in
1924. So while astronauts walked on the moon here, across the world chess
masters have been celebrating strategy and mind strenght on this day too!
